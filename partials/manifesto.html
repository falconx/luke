Make with feelingTM

I'm fascinated with consumerism. It finds its way into everything I make.

The Work

Whether its culture, fashion, lifestyles, media, technology or even peoples words and actions, we are all consumers. As a designer, I feel that I have a responsibility to respond to this.

Through visual means, sampling, remixing, criticizing, sympathizing, experimenting, I want to produce work that is both critical, challenging and relevant to today's culture.

About Luke

I come from a print background. I studied BA Design for Publishing at NUA, and moved onto MA Communication Design where I discovered that Fine art techniques and a critical edge both enabled me to grow creatively and helped push my ideas.

I am currently a digital designer at Selesti, working in Norwich with a multitude of clients on web, tablet and mobile platforms.
